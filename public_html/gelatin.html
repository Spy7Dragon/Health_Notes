GELATIN

DESCRIPTION

Gelatin is a substance sourced from the connective tissue of animals. Gelatin is produced by the partial hydrolysis of collagen.

USAGE

Normal gelatin can be added to hot liquids. Collagen hydrolysate can be added to anything. Gelatin can also be procurred from bone broth.

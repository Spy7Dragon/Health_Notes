Ketosis

KETOSIS

DESCRIPTION

A state in which the body is forced to produce ketones as energy. Ketones are derived from the breakdown of fatty acids whether dietary or body fat. Ketosis is achieved by eating a high-fat diet, fasting or starving.

USAGE

Research keto adaptation to avoid some early pitfalls.
The easiest way to find out if a person is in ketosis is testing ketone levels.
